Write about console screen.And why it is used.And how to open console screen and how to print anything on console.

Console is a screen where you display your output, and it is used for testing purposes. Before printing anything on the HTML page it is advisable to print on the console screen.

To open the Console Screen, right click on a webpage and go to inspect. On the top there would be a navigation bar, click on console.s The console.log() method writes a message to the console screen.
